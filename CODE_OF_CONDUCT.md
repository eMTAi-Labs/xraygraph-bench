# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project maintains a professional, respectful environment for everyone
who contributes, regardless of experience level, background, or affiliation.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Respectful and constructive communication
- Honest reporting of benchmark results
- Good-faith engagement with technical disagreements
- Credit to contributors and prior work

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Manipulating benchmark results or misrepresenting data
- Deliberately submitting biased benchmarks without disclosure

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers reserve the right
to remove content and restrict access as appropriate.
